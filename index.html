Welcome to HarmonyHub504

HarmonyHub504 is a music recommendation and management platform built with Flask, SQLAlchemy, and Spotipy.

Register, upload, download, and manage songs, create playlists, and receive personalized music recommendations.

Visit HarmonyHub504

Home Page

The home page of HarmonyHub504 provides a seamless user experience where you can easily navigate through your music library, explore new songs, and access your playlists.

Recommendations Page

The recommendations page offers personalized music suggestions based on your listening habits. Discover new artists and tracks tailored to your taste.

Login Page

The login page allows users to securely access their accounts. Enter your credentials to enjoy personalized music recommendations and manage your playlists.

Upload Page

On the upload page, you can easily upload your favorite songs to your library. Supported formats include MP3, WAV, and more.

Download Page

The download page lets you download songs from your library to enjoy offline. Simply select the tracks you want and download them in a few clicks.
